About Us

The new Brazilian company with ecological products

In bottle is a sustainable company, which seeks to incorporate green in nature in our daily lives and also care for the environment.
Our proposal is to create a decorative terrarium made especially from organic materials, where the customer can choose and customize according to their choice.
We will also provide a catalog with all the prices and styles on our Instagram, along with all the ordering information.
Care: just be near a well-ventilated place like a window, as you don't have to worry about humidity or any other natural factors. If you have any questions about the care of your terrarium, please contact our employee.
We will initially lose 30% of the investment value in the company, with a payback of a few months.
